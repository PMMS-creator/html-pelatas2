Getting Started

What is Pelatas?
Pelatas is a platform that connects athletes, performers, and professionals with trainers and mentors across sports, arts, wellness, and personal development. Browse profiles, compare expertise, and contact partners directly.
How does Pelatas work?
1) Search or browse partners, 2) open a profile to learn about specialty, reviews, and contact info, 3) reach out via email, phone, or social to discuss fit, availability, and pricing. In-app booking may be added in the future.
Is Pelatas free to use?
Yes. Browsing profiles and contacting trainers is free. If you book, you’ll pay the trainer directly according to their rates and policies.
Do you offer virtual sessions?
Many partners offer virtual sessions. Look for “Virtual” on a profile or ask the trainer directly.
Which categories do you support?
Sports (e.g., basketball, tennis), performance (voice, acting, music), fitness & wellness (strength, mobility, mindfulness), academics/test prep, and more. New categories are added over time.

For Trainers (Pelatas Partners)

How do I join as a trainer?
Apply on the Sign Up page. Share your experience, specialties, certifications (if applicable), and examples of results. If approved, we’ll help you publish a polished profile.
What are the requirements to join?
Demonstrated expertise, professionalism, clear communication, and relevant references or testimonials. Some fields may expect specific credentials; list these on your profile.
How do I get more clients on Pelatas?
Keep your profile complete and specific (bio, specialties, location/virtual), add reviews, post a short intro video, and respond quickly to inquiries.
Can I set my own rates and policies?
Yes. You control pricing, packages (single sessions or bundles), and cancellation terms. Display them on your profile and in client communications.
How are reviews handled?
Clients can share feedback after working with you. We monitor for respectful language and may remove content that violates our guidelines.

Booking & Payments

How do I schedule a session?
Contact the trainer via the links on their profile to confirm fit, date/time, location/virtual, and price. The trainer will share their preferred calendar and payment method.
What payment methods are supported?
Trainers typically accept standard methods (e.g., card processors, payment apps). Confirm accepted methods with the trainer before booking.
What is your cancellation or rescheduling policy?
Policies vary by trainer. Many require 24–48 hours’ notice for full refunds or credits. Review the policy on the trainer’s profile or ask directly.
Do you offer packages or subscriptions?
Many trainers offer discounted multi-session packages. Look for this on the profile or request a custom plan.

Safety & Trust

Does Pelatas vet trainers?
No. Pelatas is a visibility and connection platform — we do not verify, vet, or background-check trainers. Each trainer is responsible for their own qualifications, credentials, and safety practices.
How can I make sure a trainer is a good fit?
Review profiles and client feedback, ask questions about experience and approach, and request references or proof of credentials directly from the trainer if needed.
What if I have a concern about a trainer or session?
Save details (dates, messages, screenshots) and email team@pelatas.com. We’ll review and may remove profiles that violate our community guidelines.

Accounts & Privacy

How do I create an account?
Use Sign Up to register as a client or trainer. Trainers complete an application; clients can save favorites and manage inquiries.
How do I update or remove my profile?
Trainers can update approved profile details anytime. To pause or remove a profile, email team@pelatas.com.
How does Pelatas protect my data?
We use industry-standard security and collect only what’s needed to provide the service. A full Privacy Policy will be posted soon.

Troubleshooting & Support

I didn’t get a reply from a trainer — what should I do?
Give it 1–2 business days, then follow up. You can also message a few similar trainers to keep momentum.
My message failed to send.
Try another contact method on the profile (email/phone/social). If issues persist, contact team@pelatas.com.
Still need help?
Email us at team@pelatas.com and we’ll jump in.
