Our Story

Pelatas was founded on a simple idea: everyone deserves access to the kind of training and mentorship that creates champions.

As a cofounder and parent, I struggled to find the very best coaches for my kids — the ones who could nurture their talent and push them to grow. Pelatas was born out of that journey.
